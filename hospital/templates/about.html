About REMOTE HEALTH

Noncommunicable diseases (NCDs) kill 41 million people each year, equivalent to 74% of all deaths globally. In Odisha also non-communicable disease like heart disease, stroke, cancer, diabetes and chronic lung disease causes deaths of many people. It may be because of lack of knowledge or lack of facilities provide in time. So that we have designed a web site which helps the people to check out nearby health care facility/service . People can check if there is a health care is near their location and the facilities provide by that particular healthcare. It also provides the listing quality of health care service by tagging nearby healthcare facilities. This project can also display personal health care profile and suggestive health tips in which one can see the status of his/her health so that one can stay aware and conscious about their health.
